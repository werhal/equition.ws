Investment is not just about picking stocks, but about choosing the right combination of stocks

By choosing well-matched assets, an investor can construct a portfolio that will maximize returns for a given level of risk. Likewise, an investor can construct a portfolio with the lowest possible risk for a given desired level of return.

In both cases, the investor maximizes the same value called risk-adjusted return. This metric shows how optimal the portfolio is.

The higher the risk-adjusted return, the more generously the investor's risk is rewarded by the market.

How to calculate this metric for your future portfolio?

Equition will do the math

The app is designed to provide this metric very quickly and clearly.

You make a portfolio of any stocks, ETFs or mutual funds that you consider promising. Equition app calculates the risk-adjusted return and displays it as a single number.

Adding and removing securities immediately affects the readings. By choosing securities that increase the metric, you make the portfolio more safe, more profitable, or both.

Trend is your friend?

The metrics calculated by the app show how the securities and portfolio performed in past years or even decades. But you will have to decide for yourself whether the established trends will continue.

Equition should be not a substitute, but a complement to your own market analysis, helping you make significantly more informed decisions about your investments.

So it's not a magic pill that makes everyone instantly rich. Equition is an accurate tool for portfolio diversification by selecting securities whose combination has historically shown a high and stable profit.
